Tesla Model S - Electric Excellence

Product Features

The Tesla Model S is a premium electric sedan designed for performance, luxury, and efficiency...

  • Range: Up to 396 miles on a full charge
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 1.99 seconds
  • Top Speed: 200 mph
  • All Wheel Drive
  • Autopilot: Advanced driver-assistance features
  • Battery: 100 kWh pack
  • Interior: 17 inch cinematic display, premium sound system, and heated seats
  • Charging: 250 kW max Supercharging capability

Safety Features

  • Five-star safety rating from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA)
  • Full self driving hardware with 360-degree cameras
  • Automatic emergency braking and collision avoidance
  • Eight external cameras and 12 ultrasonic sensors
  • Side collision warning
  • Over the air software updates for improved safety performance

Performance Highlights

  • Three high-performance motors for instant torque and improved efficiency
  • Adaptive air suspension for comfort and handling
  • Carbon sleeved rotors for sustained power at high speeds
  • 0-60 mph in less than 2 seconds for the Plaid model
  • Track mode for enhanced cornering and cooling during high-speed driving
